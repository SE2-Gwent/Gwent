1. Goal of the game

    • Every match consists of 3 separate rounds.
    • To win the match, you have to win 2 rounds.

  2. Deck

    • Each deck contains 25 cards.
    • (optional) A player can always check which cards are remaining in his/her deck during each round.

  3. Hero

    • Each player has a hero with a unique ability.
    • The ability of the hero can be activated during the controlling players turn (if there is no cooldown).

  4. Graveyard

    • Cards which die during a round or which are whipped at the end of a round go to the graveyard.
    • (optional) A player can always check which cards are in his/her graveyard during each round.

  5. Hand

    • A hand can\'t hold more than 10 cards (0 = n = 10 | n = Cards in hand).

  6. Card drawing and mulligans

    • At the start of the first round both players draw 10 cards.
    • After the first round 3 cards are drawn at the beginning of each round.
    • Each card which can\'t be drawn due to the maximum hand size is converted into an additional mulligan.
    • After drawing, each player has the chance to replace n cards from his/her hand (n = 2 + additional mulligans).
    • Additional mulligans are reseted before each round.

  7. Round

    • At the first round the player who plays first is determined at random
    • Each following round the winner of the previous round plays first.
    • A round ends if both players have either passed or ended the round (see Passing).
    • After the round has ended the player with the most points wins the round (see Rows).
    • If the points are tie the winner is calculated randomly.
    • If there is a next round, the battlefield is cleared (i.e. all cards on the battlefield go to the graveyards).

  8. Turn

    • Turns are taken alternately by each player.
    • A player is allowed to pass if the conditions are met (see Passing).
    • Within each turn a player has to play exactly one card.
    • Within each turn a player is allowed to activate n abilities (0 = n = abilities which can be activated).

  9. Passing

    • A player is allowed to pass if he/ she has taken no previous actions this turn (play card, activate ability)

  10. Rows

    • The battlefield consists of rows divided into melee and ranged.
    • Each player has a melee and a ranged row.
    • The current points of a player are calculated by summing up the power of the creature-cards placed on his/her melee and ranged rows.
    • Each row can be affected by statuses which are changing the behaviour of the row.
    • The maximum number for cards on each row is 6.

  11. Card

    • Each creature-card has a power associated to it.
    • Each creature-card may have an ability (for example a creature could deal damage, when it enters the battlefield).
    • If the card is on the battlefield the power of the creature can change with the progress of a round.
    • If a creature-card on the battlefield has 0 power it dies and goes to the graveyard.
